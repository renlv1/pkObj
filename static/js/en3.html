 Product Description

 

  1. 1.Introduction of Bogle001

 

Bogle001 is a closed-end fund introduced by PK and BogleFunds. The lock-in period lasts for two years and will be officially operated on January 1, 2019 by our professional team.


From August 1st to August 31st of 2019, users could buy in the closed-end fund Bogle001 with their holding SIE. After the lock-in period, users are allowed to convert the shares at the price of 1.3 USD per SIE, or quit.

 

  1. 2.Fund manager

 

BogleFunds is a comprehensive organization of the research and development on AI, machine learning and deep learning. Financial AI, as its first project, is the research themed by AI financial arbitrage and high-frequency trading. It is turning to the machine learning, where AI systems can analyze massive data at high speed and self-improve during that process. Machines are being used to predict market trends and identify specific trades. The highly developed AI is used for financial analysis to seize the arbitrage opportunities from the variations, offering members with more valuable investment projects.

Ⅲ. The Offering and Purchase of the Fund

 

  1.   1.The offering time, offering mode and offering object of the fund

 

(1)  Offering time

 

The investor could purchase the fund during the offering period (from August 1st to August 31st of 2019). Detailed information is confirmed by the fund manager in accordance with relevant laws and regulations, and the provisions of the Contract, and is subject to the announcements from the fund manager. The fund manager has the right to shorten or to extend the raise period considering the actual selling situation in accordance with relevant procedures. The information of shortening or extending the raise will be announced on the website of the fund manager, which is deemed as the completion of the procedure for shortening or extending the raise period.

(2)  Offering mode

 

The fund is publicly offered. The fund will be promoted to specific objects via the Internet, while the raise, the shortening or extension of the raise, the basic information of the fund, and the subscription and redemption of the fund could be announced on BogleFunds website.

 

(3)  Offering object

 

The fund is offered only for SIE holders.
